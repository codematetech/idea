Climate Justice

  • Ensuring Transparency and Justice

    At IDEA, we believe that climate justice must be rooted in equity and fairness. We advocate for policies that ensure the fair distribution of resources, benefits, and burdens of climate change. This includes addressing disparities in access to clean energy, protecting the rights of Indigenous peoples, and ensuring that vulnerable communities are not disproportionately affected by climate impacts. Our work focuses on creating a level playing field where everyone has the opportunity to thrive in a sustainable environment.Home, About, and FAQ.

    Ensuring Transparency and Accountability Civic accountability is crucial for effective climate action. IDEA advocates for transparent, inclusive, and accountable governance in climate-related policies and initiatives. We work to ensure that communities have a voice in decision-making processes and that governments and corporations are held accountable for their environmental impacts. Through advocacy, education, and community organizing, we promote civic engagement and empower individuals to take action for climate justice.
